# Simple Interest Flowchart 💰

## Problem

Calculate Simple Interest (SI) given Principal (P), Rate (R), and Time (T).

---

## Formula

SI = (P × R × T) / 100

---

## Steps (Algorithm Thinking)

1. Start
2. Input P, R, T
3. Calculate SI = (P × R × T) / 100
4. Display SI
5. End
