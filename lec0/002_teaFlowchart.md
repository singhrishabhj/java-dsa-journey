# Tea Flowchart ☕

## Problem

Make a cup of tea step by step.

---

## Steps (Algorithm Thinking)

1. Start
2. Add water to pan
3. Boil water
4. Add tea leaves
5. Add sugar
6. Add milk
7. Boil for some time
8. Tea ready?

   * Yes → Serve tea
   * No → Boil more
9. End

## Understanding

* Sequence matters (order is important)
* Decision point: "Tea Ready?"
* Loop used if tea is not ready

---

## Mistakes I made

* Forgot decision step
* Did not handle "No" case properly
* Made steps unclear

---

## Key Takeaway

Every real-life task can be broken into logical steps using flowcharts.
